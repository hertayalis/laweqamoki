I remember stumbling upon this song, “Insert Cash,” by Alex Schor at a friend’s house. As a DJ, I’m always on the lookout for fresh and unique tracks to play at parties, and this song immediately caught my attention.

The Lyrics

The lyrics of “Insert Cash” are bold, unapologetic, and brimming with confidence. Alex Schor delivers his verses with a charismatic flair, showcasing his rap skills and playful wordplay.

In the chorus, he references Kanye West’s hit song “Gold Digger,” but adds his own twist to it. He explains how he’s not looking for a long-term commitment with someone, but rather desires a temporary connection. The line “Insert cash or select payment type” reflects his intention to enjoy the moment without any emotional attachment.

Alex Schor’s verses are filled with clever metaphors and self-assured statements. He proudly claims to be so good-looking that if he got married, he would be considered the wife, highlighting his confidence and self-assuredness. The line “Mobilize, shoot her ass, upit the pressure” suggests his determination and drive to succeed in the music industry, as he aims to leave a lasting impression.

He also showcases his rap skills with witty wordplay and bold imagery. Lines such as “Shot my shot at that bitch and it airballed” and “I’m an oil spill, I talk slick” demonstrate his ability to create catchy and impactful lines.

The Message

While “Insert Cash” may seem light-hearted and playful on the surface, it actually touches upon themes of self-confidence, ambition, and not conforming to societal expectations. Alex Schor boldly expresses his individuality and refuses to be limited by others’ opinions. He embraces who he is and showcases his unique style and approach to music.

Furthermore, the song also explores the idea of temporary connections and living in the moment. It challenges the traditional notion of romance and emphasizes the importance of valuing experiences without getting entangled in emotional attachments.

About the Artist

Alex Schor, the creator of “Insert Cash,” is a talented artist who not only produces his own music but also writes his own lyrics. His ability to captivate listeners with his catchy beats and charismatic delivery sets him apart as a rising star in the rap genre.

“Insert Cash” is part of his upcoming album, “She Want Me,” scheduled to be released in 2023. With this song, Alex Schor showcases his unique style and fearlessly ventures into uncharted territories, leaving an indelible mark on the music industry.

Final Thoughts

“Insert Cash” by Alex Schor is a vibrant and energetic track that encompasses elements of confidence, ambition, and unconventional romance. Its catchy hooks and clever wordplay make it an exciting addition to any playlist.

As a DJ, I’m always thrilled to discover artists who bring something new and refreshing to the table, and Alex Schor certainly delivers with “Insert Cash.” It’s a song that embraces individuality, challenges societal norms, and encourages listeners to live life to the fullest.
