YouTube Star and Makeup Artist Mel Thompson Has Died

Mel Thompson, a YouTube star and makeup artist, has died at the age of 35. Details on what her husband has shared about her tragic passing.

Makeup artist and YouTube personality Mel Thompson has passed away at the age of 35. Her husband, Eric Thompson, shared the news of the beauty influencer's death in an Instagram statement on her page.

"Mel sadly passed away yesterday," Eric — who is nicknamed "Puffin" — wrote on Sept. 27. "It's really hard to just choose 10 photos. We lost a beautiful person. I just wanted to show the smiles she brought. I've had to answer so many texts of people just checking in on her without even knowing of her passing. It's great to see how loved she was. And she loved all you right back."

"I'll continue to love her and miss her forever. She was such a pillar for our family. The kids would talk to her non stop and she was constantly talking with them and trying to help them with their stuff. And no matter how bad she felt, she would still rub my back when I came and jumped on the bed next to her while she was working tirelessly to put out content," he continued.

"Everybody knows how talented she was as an artist and with her helpful knowledge, but those closest to her know how truly wonderful she was. She would have done anything to help anyone without a second thought," he concluded. "I wish I had her back."

YouTube star Mel Thompson has died — details on her career and her family life.

The Tennessee-based makeup artist joined YouTube in 2013, and her channel has more than 170,000 subscribers. During her time on the video sharing platform, Mel posted nearly 1,230 vlogs.

The late influencer largely shared makeup tutorials, product hauls, and reviews on her page. Her final video, "WAYNE GOSS MINE 4K" was posted just two days before her death (though it's unclear when exactly she filmed it).

Outside of her vlogging career, Mel was a married mom of four.

In May 2020, she celebrated Mother's Day with a photo of her brood.

"I grew up always saying I would never have kids. Here I am today with 4 and I wouldn't trade places with anyone. I'm so grateful to have such amazing kids and an amazing family. Being a mom is both rewarding and extremely frustrating at times... but ALWAYS more rewarding," she wrote. "To my babies, (you'll always be babies to me) I love you with all my heart. Mom, thank you for teaching me how to be a good mother..."

What was Mel Thompson's cause of death?

The late YouTube star's husband did not share how she died, though his statement did make mention of her not feeling well. Mel herself had previously discussed being sick in several of her videos.

Mel's cause of death remains publicly unknown.

Others in the YouTube beauty community paid tribute to Mel online.

Vlogger and makeup artist Robert Welsh wrote, "This is such heartbreaking news. Mel was such a wonderful person. I'm so deeply sorry for your loss."

"I am in utter shock," Nikkia Joy added, along with the broken heart emoji. "This is the most heartbreaking news [cry face emoji] I am so so sorry for your loss, the world will not be the same without Mel."

Comedian Jamie French shared that Mel was "such a sweet and wonderful, beautiful person" in a comment.
