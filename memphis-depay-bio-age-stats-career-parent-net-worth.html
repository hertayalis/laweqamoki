Who is Memphis Depay?

Memphis Depay is a professional footballer from the Netherlands. He plays as an attacker for the Netherlands national team and the La Liga giants Barcelona FC.

Football Career of Memphis Depay

Memphis began his football career at the age of 8. He started with his home youth team Moordrecht in Holland. He moved to Sparta Rotterdam in 2003 and then to PSV in 2006. Memphis impressed his coaches in his first season. He made eight appearances- all coming on as a substitute- and scoring 3 goals. When he turned 12 years, he caught the attention of big clubs in the Eredivisie; Ajax, Feyenoord, and PSV. He stayed with PSV. Memphis had a great season in 2014/2015. He was the top scorer with 22 goals and PSV was crowned the Eredivisie Champions. 

On 27th May 2015, Memphis signed a four-year contract with Manchester United under the management of Luis Van Gaal, at a fee of £25 million. He didn’t get much playing time with Manchester United. He made 53 appearances in 3 years for United scoring only 7 goals.

On 20th January 2017, Memphis signed a four and half year deal with the French Ligue 1 club Lyon. The transfer was at a fee of £17 million. He had an outstanding spell at Lyon. He was compared with the likes of David Beckham.

On 19th June 2021, Memphis moved to the Spanish La Liga side FC Barcelona on a free transfer two-year contract.

Memphis has represented the Netherlands at all the youth levels. He was part of the under-17 team which won the 2011 European Championship in Serbia. On 15th October 2013, Memphis made his first International debut and since then has always represented the Netherlands in all football competitions.

He is currently part of the 26-man squad in the 2022 Qatar World Cup.
