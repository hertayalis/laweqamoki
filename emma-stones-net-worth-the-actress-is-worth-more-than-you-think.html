The Actress Is Worth More Than You Think

July 08, 2024
blog

Emma Stone has enjoyed a successful career in Hollywood, and her net worth of $30 million certainly reflects this fact (via Celebrity Net Worth). Stone started her acting career in television, according to her IMDb page, landing small roles in shows such as Medium, The Suite Life of Zack and Cody, and Malcolm in the Middle. By 2007, however, Stone made her way into the film industry, landing a starring role in the comedy Superbad. In 2009, she starred in another hit film, Zombieland, which helped put the actress on the comedy scene. 

Of course, Stone's career really took off once she landed the lead role as Olive in Easy A. The film was met with both commercial and critical success, and from there, Stone continued to land hot roles. From Crazy, Stupid, Love to The Amazing Spider-Man to The Help, Stone spent much of the early 2010s starring in some of the biggest movies to hit the big screen. In 2017, the actress won her first Oscar for her performance in La La Land, which she starred in alongside Ryan Gosling. 

With her latest movie Cruella set to hit theaters in May 2021 (via Forbes), Stone is certainly not slowing down anytime soon, so we can only expect to see her already impressive net worth continue to grow.
