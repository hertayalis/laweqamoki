Blake Lively Trolls Ryan Reynolds with Favorite Vancouver Items

August 03, 2024
blog

When it comes to Hollywood couples, Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds definitely don't take themselves too seriously. In fact, the two stars regularly roast one another on social media, and Lively's latest joke about Reynolds is seriously good.

In her Instagram Stories, Lively decided to share a list of four of her "favorite things in the world" from Vancouver, Canada, which just so happens to be where husband, Reynolds, is from. In the first photo she shared, Lively revealed that she loves Vancouver-made ice cream, donuts, and pie. While fans were likely expecting Reynolds to be Lively's fourth "favorite thing," the Gossip Girl star had a surprise in mind.

In a subsequent picture, Lively shared a picture of a dessert from Ask For Luigi, an Italian restaurant in Vancouver. She captioned the photo, "Who did you think I was gonna say??" and posted a Deadpool GIF in reference to her hubby's famous role. Leave it to Lively to choose dessert over Reynolds.

Instagram

This is far from the first time that Lively has publicly trolled her husband. In September, she joked that Reynolds gave their dog special treatment, after the actor shared a birthday tribute for the dog, but not for his wife. She commented on the post, "This is almost as sweet as the birthday post you did for me this year," to which Reynolds replied, "I baked you a cake."

And in October, when Reynolds ate his "birthday pie" before blowing out the candles, Lively savagely joked, "Happy Birthday. I honestly can’t believe we’re still married."
