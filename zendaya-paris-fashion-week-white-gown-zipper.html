Zendaya Wore a Daring White Gown at Paris Fashion Week

June 08, 2024
blog

Zendaya is about to put zipper gowns on the map.

The Emmy-winning actor made a head-turning entrance at today’s Louis Vuitton Spring/Summer 2024 runway show during Paris Fashion Week.

For the star-studded event, the Euphoria star arrived in an ethereal white gown, which featured a slender form-fitting silhouette that hugged her torso before flaring out into a delicate train right below her hips, as well as bulky gold buckles and caramel leather panels adorning the straps. Most notable, however, was the detail fixed at the gown’s center: a gold two-way zipper with white leather leaf-shaped pull tabs. The pull tabs were strategically positioned to reveal a plunging V-neckline and a high center leg slit.

Zendaya coordinated the look with white satin pumps and more gold accessories, including an array of gold bangle bracelets, chunky gold rings, and small gold hoop earrings.

Louis Vuitton tapped Zendaya to be its newest celebrity brand ambassador earlier this year. Thus far, the Emmy-winning actor has starred in a campaign for the house’s iconic Capucines handbags and has repped the brand at various public events and outings.

Indeed, her last major public appearance was to attend Louis Vuitton’s Spring/Summer 2024 menswear show in June, where she joined the likes of Beyoncé and Jay-Z on the front row. At the time, Zendaya dazzled in a baggy collared blouse and matching flared trousers, both of which were entirely covered in light blue, yellow, and black sequins that composed an architectural graphic illustration. She finished off the look with brown pumps, a black bag, and layered diamond choker and pendant necklaces.
